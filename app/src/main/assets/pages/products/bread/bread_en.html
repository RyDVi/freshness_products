Definition by appearance. Bread should not be crushed or cracked. It cannot be black or green. It should have “its own unique aroma”, and not the smell of mold.

    

If the bread contains an expired product, then when it is compressed, a slight smell of mold is felt. If you press on the bread, and it is not restored back, then this loaf is not baked.

    

When choosing a baguette, if they knock, the sound should be empty. In this case, it is normal for use.
